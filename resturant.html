......WELCOME TO THE OUR RESTURANT......




Mountain Peak

Mountain Peak Restaurant is a cozy and elegant place that offers a variety of dishes and drinks for its customers. The restaurant is located on the top floor of a skyscraper, which gives a stunning view of the city skyline and the mountains.

Sky lounge

Sky Lounge Restaurant is a modern and stylish place that offers a variety of dishes and drinks for its customers. The restaurant is located on the rooftop of a hotel, which gives a breathtaking view of the city lights and the stars.

The Greenery



The Greenery Restaurant is a cozy and elegant place that offers fresh and organic food and beverages for its customers. The restaurant is surrounded by lush greenery and has a natural and relaxing ambiance. The restaurant serves a variety of dishes, such as salads, soups, sandwiches, pasta, pizza, and more, made with locally sourced ingredients and herbs.
