Meepe Branch

The Meepe branch of the Mountain View Nature Resort is beautiful resort, a 3.5-star hotel that is located in the mountains of Meepe, Sri Lanka.You can relax and swim in the infinity pool that overlooks the city and the mountains, or test your skills and courage in the rope course challenge that has different obstacles while suspended in the air.

Ella Branch

The Ella branch of the Mountain View Nature Resort is also a beautiful resort, a 3.5-star hotel that is set in Ella amid a stunning landscape.You can also camp in the site and have a bonfire at night, or dine in the restaurant that has a view deck that offers a panoramic view of the surroundings.

Haputale Branch

The Haputale branch of the Mountain View Nature Resort is also a beautiful resort, a 3.5-star hotel that is located on the edge of a mountain in Haputale, Sri Lanka. The hotel offers a range of facilities and services for guests who want to enjoy the scenic views of nature and experience the thrill of adventure.
