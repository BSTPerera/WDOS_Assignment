Rope Course challenge

The rope course challenge is an adventure activity that involves moving from one platform to another while suspended in the air by ropes, wires, or cables. The challenge tests your skills and courage as you traverse different obstacles, such as bridges, ladders, swings, nets, and zip lines.The rope course challenge can be fun and exciting, but also safe and secure, as you are equipped with a harness and a helmet, and supervised by trained staff.

Archery Range


The archery range challenge is an adventure activity that involves shooting arrows at a target with a bow. The challenge tests your skills and accuracy as you aim and fire at different distances, angles, and conditions. The archery range challenge can be fun and exciting, but also safe and secure, as you are equipped with a bow, arrows, a quiver, and an arm guard, and supervised by trained staff.




ATV Riding

The ATV riding challenge is an adventure activity that involves driving an all-terrain vehicle (ATV) on a dirt track or a natural trail. The challenge tests your skills and speed as you maneuver your ATV over bumps, turns, and obstacles. The ATV riding challenge can be fun and exciting, but also safe and secure, as you are equipped with a helmet, gloves, and boots, and supervised by trained staff.
